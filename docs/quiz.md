# AI Textbook Quiz

Welcome to the interactive quiz system of the AI Textbook! This feature allows you to test your knowledge as you progress through the textbook content.

## How It Works

The Quiz Agent automatically generates questions based on the content of each topic covered in the textbook. After each topic section, you can take an interactive quiz that includes:

- Questions tailored to the specific content you've learned
- Three difficulty levels: Beginner, Normal, and High
- Immediate feedback on your answers
- Explanations to help reinforce learning
- Score tracking and summary

## Difficulty Levels

1. **Beginner**: Fundamental concepts and basic definitions
2. **Normal**: Core principles and applications
3. **High**: Advanced understanding and analytical thinking

## Using the Quiz System

1. Navigate to the [Quiz page](/quiz) from the main navigation menu
2. Select a topic you've studied from the available options
3. Choose your preferred difficulty level
4. Answer each question one at a time
5. Receive immediate feedback on your answers
6. Review the complete summary after finishing the quiz

## Benefits

- Reinforces learning through active recall
- Identifies areas where you may need further study
- Tracks your progress through the textbook content
- Makes learning more engaging and interactive

Try the quiz system after reading each chapter to enhance your understanding and retention of the material!
